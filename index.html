La boulangerie

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the goodness and pleasure of eating baked goods, desserts and confections. But gluten-free people often find themselves at a loss trying to find places that can replicate these desserts in a gluten free version. La Boulangerie found its niche in making baked goods of the same quality but using gluten free and vegan ingredients that everyone can enjoy! Our philosophy is baked from the freshest of ingredients.
